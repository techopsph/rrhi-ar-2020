About the Cover

Our cover features cashiers from Robinsons Supermarket at the height of community lockdowns. When the COVID-19 outbreak reached the Philippines, our essential formats, namely our supermarkets, drugstores and convenience stores, remained open to serve our customers.

The 2020 Annual and Sustainability Report is entitled “Salute to Frontliners” in recognition of the efforts of our employees during these trying times. We are grateful for the dedication they have shown, and we continue to value the contributions of our people in our journey as one of the leading retailers in the Philippines.
